Intro to Ciphers

Ciphers 101:

This website lets you encode and decode messages with 5 different ciphers. The first four are Substitution Ciphers: ciphers which replace each unit of a message with a unit of ciphertext. We've included several substitution ciphers than can be easily implemented with pen and paper, as well as binary XOR, a substitution cipher usually implemented with a computer.

Since substitution ciphers are easily broken, we've also provided you the option to encrypt your messages with AES Crypto, a much more secure encryption method.

If you want to learn more about any of these ciphers, you can check out the links above for explanations and demos. Happy encrypting!
